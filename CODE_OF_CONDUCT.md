# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect respectful, constructive collaboration from all contributors.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate professionally.
- Give actionable technical feedback.
- Respect differing viewpoints.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Disruptive behavior that prevents collaboration.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary.

## Reporting

Report issues privately via the contact listed in `SECURITY.md`.
